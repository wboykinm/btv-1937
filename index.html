About

This map compares aerial views of Burlington's South End between 1937 and 2012, offering a window into patterns of residential and industrial development over nearly eight decades. Drag the slider on the left to toggle between old and new.

  • 1937 Imagery acquired by the Sanborn Fire Insurance co. and archived by the UVM Baily-Howe Library
  • 2012 Imagery provided by Mapbox
  • Street and Reference data by OpenStreetmap Contributors
  • Application by Geosprocket
